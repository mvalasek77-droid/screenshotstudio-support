Frequently Asked Questions

Does Screenshot Studio send my screenshots to a server?

No. All screenshot processing happens locally on your device. No images, data, or settings are ever uploaded to any server. Your screenshots remain completely under your control.

What data does the app collect?

Screenshot Studio only accesses the photos and screenshots you explicitly select within the app, and saves your in-app preferences (like favorite device frames and colors). Nothing is sent off-device. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Does the app include analytics or tracking?

No. Screenshot Studio contains no third-party analytics SDKs, no advertising trackers, and no usage telemetry. We believe your data is your business — literally.

Which device frames are supported?

Screenshot Studio supports frames for current Apple devices including:

  • iPhone 16 / 16 Pro / 16 Pro Max
  • iPhone 15 / 15 Pro / 15 Pro Max
  • iPad Pro (M4)
  • iPad Air (M2)
  • And more
How do I export my screenshots?

Once you've composed your screenshot, simply tap the export button to save the final image directly to your photo library. You can then upload it to App Store Connect via your browser or Transporter app.

Is Screenshot Studio suitable for children?

Yes. The app is rated 4+ on the App Store and contains no content inappropriate for children. It also does not collect any data from users of any age.

I'm having an issue. How do I get help?

We're here to help! Email us at support@mikevalasek.com and we'll get back to you as soon as possible. Please include your device model and iOS version for the fastest support.
